Our business ethos:

Here at Busy Bees, we are committed to conserve our natural habitats. This loss of traditional habitat is having a devastating effect on Bee and Butterfly populations throughout the developed world. Biodiversity is crucial for many reasons and wildflowers are crucial to biodiversity.
